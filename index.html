Merge, Explore, and Build Your Candy Empire in Candy Critters!

Lead a team of adorable Chomplets on a delightful adventure through Sweetopia! Merge, recruit, and expand your candy-clearing operation while uncovering hidden treasures and growing your profits.

Advantages

  • Unique Merge & Idle Gameplay

    Merge critters to increase their power and clear obstacles faster.

  • Endless Exploration

    Unlock new regions of Sweetopia, each with its own challenges and hidden treasures.

  • Strategic Planning

    Balance merging, upgrading, and deployment to optimize candy-clearing efficiency.

  • Idle Progression

    Earn rewards even while offline - progress continues while you're away.

  • Vibrant and Engaging World

    Colorful visuals, adorable characters, and a lively soundtrack keep the experience exciting.

  • Upgrade and Invest

    Collect upgrade cards to boost your speed, profits, and Chomplet efficiency.

Benefits

  • Relaxing Yet Addictive Gameplay

    Simple mechanics, but deep strategy keeps players engaged for hours.

  • Unlockable Content

    New critters, power-ups, and candy factories unlock as you progress.

  • Compete and Compare

    Track your progress against other players and aim for the highest efficiency.

About us

The Ultimate Merge & Idle Experience in a Candy Wonderland

In Candy Critters, you become the leader of a team of adorable Chomplets who clear candy-covered lands in Sweetopia. Your goal is to merge, optimize, and expand your operation while uncovering hidden coins, gems, and ancient candy factories.

In Candy Critters, you become the leader of a team of adorable Chomplets who clear candy-covered lands in Sweetopia. Your goal is to merge, optimize, and expand your operation while uncovering hidden coins, gems, and ancient candy factories.

  • Recruit and Merge - Grow your team by hiring new Chomplets and combining identical ones to boost power.

  • Clear Obstacles & Find Treasures - Remove candy debris to uncover rewards and unlock new areas.

  • Explore and Rebuild - Restore long-lost candy factories to boost your earnings.

  • Optimize for Maximum Efficiency - Decide whether to merge critters, unlock upgrades, or deploy them for quicker results.

With its blend of strategy, progression, and adorable characters, Candy Critters offers a relaxing yet deeply engaging experience.
